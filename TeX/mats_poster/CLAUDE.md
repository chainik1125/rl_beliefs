## Poster Layout Guidelines

1. **Layout integrity after every change**: After each edit to the poster, verify:
   - No boxes are overflowing their allocated space
   - The three-band structure is preserved: 2 boxes in band 1, 3 boxes in band 2, 2 boxes in band 3
   - No other pathologies (overlapping elements, clipped text, broken spacing) were introduced by the change
   - If a change to the basic geometry (band sizes, box counts, overall dimensions) seems necessary, **ask the user before making it**

2. **Adapt images to the poster, not the other way around**: Images, graphs, and figures should be regenerated or resized to fit the poster layout. Do not restructure the poster to accommodate image dimensions. Most graphs can be regenerated at the right size.
